About

I joined Homestead to help build out the retention service offering. The team was already doing thoughtful work on the acquisition side, and I'd been around long enough to know that acquisition and retention aren't separate disciplines — they're the same conversation, told at different points in the customer relationship.

Today I'm Director of Strategy. I oversee our retention team — 50+ specialists working across 100+ DTC brands — and work with them to make sure we're delivering results for our clients. This is also where I write about what I'm figuring out along the way.
